A simple Tetris game, created by Nathan J. Brown, December 2018. This is version 1.0

Tetris is played on a 10 by 20 grid. Shapes called Tetriminos fall from the top of the grid and land at the bottom or on other Tetrominos.

You're job is to rotate and move the pieces so they slot together. A good strategy will help.

As time progresses the game speeds up. Good luck!
